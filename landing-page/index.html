Powerful Features

Everything you need to scale support

AI Knowledge Base (RAG)

Upload PDFs or paste raw text. Documents are intelligently chunked and stored as vector embeddings using `pgvector` for hyper-accurate retrieval.

Intelligent Web Crawler

Automatically crawl and scrape pages from your base URL. Keep your AI trained on your latest website content without manual uploads.

Seamless Agent Handoff

When the AI can't answer, chats escalate directly to a human agent via real-time Socket.IO routing with full conversation history preserved.

Effortless Integration

Install in less than 60 seconds

No complex SDKs, no dependency hell. Just drop a single script tag into the `` or `` of your website, and the chatbot widget automatically initializes and binds to your backend knowledge base.

  • Works with WordPress, Shopify, React, & Vanilla HTML
  • Zero impact on your initial page load speed
  • Highly customizable via dashboard settings
index.html 
<!-- 1. Add the widget container -->
<div id="KnowBridge-chat-root"></div>

<!-- 2. Load the script -->
<script src="https://your-domain.com/chat-widget.bundle.js"></script>

<!-- 3. Initialize it -->
<script>
  window.KnowBridgeChat.init({
    apiUrl: 'https://api.your-domain.com'
  });
</script>
